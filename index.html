Overview

How to Use

Select your active XP boosts above. The site will automatically recalculate all XP and GP rates across every tab. Your settings are saved locally in your browser.

Locked Tasks

If you don't have the Slayer level for a creature, assign it to the Block list on the Prefer / Block tab. This removes it from the Filter view so your optimal task list stays accurate.

Data Sources

Kill rates provided by the community. If you have specific KPH videos that match or exceed current data, contact .saltea on Discord.

Feedback

For suggestions or issues, message .saltea on Discord.
