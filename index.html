Overview

How to Use

Select your active XP boosts above. The site will automatically recalculate all XP and GP rates across every tab. Your settings are saved locally in your browser.

Editable KPH

On the Slayer Tasks tab, click any KPH value to edit it with your own kills/hour data. Custom values are saved locally and persist between sessions.

Data Sources

Kill rates provided by the community. If you have specific KPH videos that match or exceed current data, contact .saltea on Discord so we can include it in the data.

Feedback

For suggestions or issues, message .saltea on Discord.
